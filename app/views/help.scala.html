Creating an appointment

Dashboard

Within the dashboard, both students and coaches can quickly create a new general appointment with the Quick-Schedule button located on top right corner of the dashboard.

Coach List

The coaches list will display all coaches and allow quick-scheduling to a specific coach via a button in the manage section of the page. Each coach will have a button corresponding to them.

Managing Appointments

Dashboard

The dashboard will display your next 5 appointments, along with a week-view calendar of all appointments this week. The next 5 appointment list will also contain a button to view the appointment list.

Appointment List

Within the appointment list, coaches and students can access and alter important information around each appointment.

Students: Students can easily view all of their upcoming & past appointments, as well as cancel any upcoming ones using the appointment controls. An additional button will also allow students to email their coach quickly.

Coaches: In the appointment list coaches can quickly email students, cancel appointments, as well as add coach notes to each appointment. Coach notes are not visible by students, but are the equivalent to session logs, and allow coaches to stay on top of topics and review what was discussed or completed in previous appointments.

Services & Appointment Types

Services

Services are defined by each individual learning center. They are custom attributes that can be attached to specific coaches, and used to filter available appointment slots. For example, a coach may be able to provide help with "Writing", where other coaches may not. This "Writing" service will appear in the dropdown "Service" for creating a new appointment. Another example may be to create classes as services, if coaches are able to provide help in a class such as CS3000, where other coaches may not have taken it yet.

Appointment Types

Like services, appointment types can be set by each individual learning center. The three most commonly used appointment types consist of One-Time and Weekly. Appointment types are used to determine what frequency or mode the appointment will take place. For instance, some appointment availabilities may only be created to facilitate a weekly spot. Another example may be Online appointments, where coaches can mark availability times to allow online submissions only.
